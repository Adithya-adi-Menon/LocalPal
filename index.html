Description

LocalPal is a location-based Android application built using Flutter that strengthens local community and bring people with similar interests together. For example, you could be looking for someone to build a PC with, or go on a hike with, or listen to music with together. You can then search on the map for local events and people who are nearby, and you can join a chatroom to find a group that shares the same passion as you.

Features

Personalized Content

We will recommend content & event postings based on your preferences and profile info, so you won't be receiving irrelevant information.

Video Call Supported

We support video calling so that users can meet online and have more means of interacting with one another.

Geo-coding Included

Regardless of who you are, there are always people in your community who are passionate about the same things as you.

Event Based

We encourage users to find local events listed in the app, as well as create their own through making public posts.
